Information about school

«More than» Music School is a modern, stylish, and inspiring space for vocal and instrumental education in Cincinnati. Located just three minutes from Downtown, our school offers easy parking, a fully equipped in-house recording studio, certified instructors, and a warm, creative atmosphere that helps every student feel at home.
We are not just a school — we are a place where music becomes a lifestyle.
Our mission is to develop musical talent at any age and level, from complete beginners to advanced performers. We focus on practical skills, confident stage presence, and real artistic growth. Students learn to express emotions through music, discover their unique style, and gain experience through live performances and studio recording sessions.
At «More than», we believe that everyone has a voice worth hearing — and we help bring it to life.
